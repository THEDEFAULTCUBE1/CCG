YOUR OBJECTIVE
Codes have been concealed throughout the operational area — scattered across the forest, the roads connecting the bunker to the outside world, the airbase, the bunker entrance, and deep within its underground levels. Find them. Enter them here. Each code unlocks a piece of the classified archive, a reward, or a hidden secret no one else will see.
THE CCG BUNKER
A single classified structure that expands underground through multiple sublevels. Road access points cut through the surrounding forest, connecting the bunker to the outside world. TRB Security Division controls all entry points with extreme prejudice. Below Sublevel 3, access requires Omega clearance. Below Sublevel 4, no reliable signals have been recorded. What exists at the bottom remains unknown — or at least, officially unacknowledged.
TRB SECURITY DIVISION
The TRB Security Division is the armed intelligence and enforcement wing responsible for CCG Bunker protection. They monitor all terminal sessions, all code entries, all operative movements throughout the facility. Within the bunker, their authority is absolute. They are allies — as long as your clearance is valid.
